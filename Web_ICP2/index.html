Steps to Playing Music

To become a worship musician, there a few things to keep in mind:

  1. Practice - Just because you are playing for a community does not mean you don't put in the work. You should play your best, and that means you should practice.
  2. It's Not About You - When playing for ministry, don't try to solo or draw attention to yourself. You are there to help people worship God, so don't distract them.
  3. Actually Worship - God has given you a gift and you have the opportunity to use your talents for Him. Don't be so focused on being perfect that you forget why you are playing.
